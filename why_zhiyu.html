    YOUR PERSONAL NUTRITIONIST

    Eating well is the cornerstone of good health. With the help of our certified nutritionists, you can customize a meal plan geared specifically towards you. Our meals satisfy diets ranging 1200-3000+ daily calories. We can accommodate a variety of dietary requirements, fitness goals and personal preferences.

    YOUR PERSONAL CHEF

    Our executive chefs and their team create weekly menus offering over 100 meals and snacks to choose from that are convenient, nutritious and affordable. One size never fits all so why should your meals?

    CATERED TO YOU

    At Zhi yu, our customers come first. We take great pride in making sure every meal comes from the freshest ingredients. You will always get what you ordered, when you ordered it and where you wanted it delivered. No grocery, no cooking, no cleaning.
